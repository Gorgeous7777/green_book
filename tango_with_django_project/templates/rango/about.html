What is Green Book

The Green Book is a lifestyle platform for young people, founded by Team Good Job.
The Green Book takes "Inspire Lives to share and discover the wonders of the world" as its mission.
Users can record life details through pictures and texts, share lifestyles, and form interactions based on interests.
